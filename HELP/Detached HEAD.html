What is Detached HEAD?

If you go back in history, and checkout a commit, you are recommended only to look around, and make experimental changes. If you have made a change, and want to return to the top of your working branch, your work may be lost.

What to do?

In above situation, Pragma-git gives you a chance to save your work as a stash, or delete. If you stash your work, it can be recalled in any branch, by the stash pop command .
